How we met

There was a green field (commonly known as a pitch), and a ball. Valentina wouldn't pass the ball so Deeple decided to have a friendly word. Val had no time for chit chat and would constantly run off. Later it turns out she was shy and having just moved to the UK, was still adjusting to life away from home. Eventually, Valentina tried Deeple's cooking and was so impressed. Little did Val know, that after that, she would always be doing the cooking!
